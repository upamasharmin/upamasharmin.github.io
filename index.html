Biography

I am currently working as a Data Scientist (Business & Sales Analytics) at ACI PLC, where I explore data to uncover meaningful patterns that support strategic decision-making across business and sales domains. My work involves developing analytical solutions using Python, ML-based solutions, designing efficient data pipelines, performing sales forecasting and building clear, insightful visualizations.

Before joining ACI, I worked as a Business Intelligence Analyst at EDOTCO Bangladesh, where I developed full-cycle data projects, automated reporting ecosystems and solved real operational challenges through data-driven approaches.

Beyond industry work, my research interests lie in Natural Language Processing (NLP), Human–Computer Interaction (HCI) and Multimodal Learning as well as the interdisciplinary spaces where these domains intersect. I am particularly passionate about exploring how language, vision and human-centered design can be combined to build more intuitive, context-aware and impactful AI systems.
